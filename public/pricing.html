Common questions

What happens to my stake if I skip?
85% of your stake goes directly to the charity you chose when you signed up. The remaining 15% covers LockEye's operating costs. If you show up and verify, you keep every dollar.
Do I need to pay anything to try the app?
No. The Free plan requires no card to sign up. You only put money on the line as your stake for each session, and you get that back if you show up.
What is the $24.99 Pro price exactly?
It's a lifetime access fee, paid once. You're not subscribing — you're buying into the founder tier at a reduced rate ($24.99 vs $99.99 after launch). Pay once, use LockEye Pro forever.
When does the app launch?
We're building now. The waitlist is open and we'll roll out in waves, starting with the first 2,000 founders. You'll get an email the moment your wave opens.
What gyms does LockEye work with?
Any gym with a physical location. LockEye uses GPS coordinates, so you just add your gym's address and the app handles the rest. No integration with the gym is required.
