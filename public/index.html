Questions?

Everything you need to know

LockEye is a new kind of gym accountability. Here’s what people usually ask.

You set a stake per session — say $30. If you show up and verify via GPS + selfie, the money stays in your wallet. If you skip, 85% goes to the charity you chose and 15% covers platform costs. No one profits from your skip.

85% of your stake is sent to your selected charity. The remaining 15% covers payment processing, verification infrastructure, and operating costs. Your streak resets, but your money still does good.

GPS confirms you’re within range of your gym location, and liveness detection ensures the selfie is a real person — not a photo or video. Spoofing both simultaneously is orders of magnitude harder than just going to the gym.

You choose from a curated set of high-impact organisations including St. Jude Children’s Research, Feeding America, ASPCA, and Khan Academy. We add new charities regularly based on member votes.

Yes. The Free plan gives you the core experience: staking money, GPS + selfie check-ins, streak tracking, and charity routing. LockEye Pro ($24.99 lifetime) adds unlimited goals, grace passes, streak revival, group challenges, and a founder badge.

Pro members get one grace pass per month — you can skip without forfeiting. On the Free plan, you can pause your goals at any time. Your streak freezes, not resets.
